## Amazon-Style Future Press Release

**ProofKind Launches AI-Powered Professional Profiles That Are Built From Real Career Evidence**

**New platform helps professionals turn scattered documents, projects, writing, recommendations, and public work into a living, proof-backed profile they control**

**London, UK - September 16, 2026** - Today, ProofKind announced the launch of its AI-powered professional identity platform, designed to help experienced professionals build richer, more credible public profiles from their real body of work.

Instead of asking users to manually write another static CV or profile page, ProofKind connects to approved data sources such as Google Drive, blogs, GitHub, LinkedIn exports, work samples, journals, performance reviews, psychometric reports, and public web sources. Its AI agent inventories, classifies, and synthesizes the material into a private professional memory. From there, users can ask questions, reconstruct career timelines, identify recurring strengths, prepare for reviews or interviews, evaluate opportunities, generate role-specific CV drafts, and approve evidence-backed claims for public use.

"Professionals are more than a job title and a list of employers," said the ProofKind founder. "Their best evidence is usually scattered across old CVs, project documents, presentations, blog posts, recommendations, and public product work. ProofKind helps people turn that evidence into a profile that is richer, more trustworthy, and easier to explore."

Each ProofKind profile includes an approved public page and an AI-powered fit advisor. Visitors can ask whether the person is a good fit for a role, project, advisory need, or business problem. The public AI can only use owner-approved claims and public-safe summaries, while the private AI workbench and professional coach remain available only to the profile owner.

"Before ProofKind, I had years of useful experience buried in files and old documents," said an early user. "Now I can ask my own career history questions privately, prepare for important career moments, and share a public profile that helps people understand my real strengths without sending another generic PDF."

ProofKind is built for consultants, executives, senior technologists, product leaders, advisors, creators, and specialists whose professional value and growth cannot be captured in a traditional resume alone.

ProofKind is available today for early access users.
